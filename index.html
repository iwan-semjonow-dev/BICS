BICS

Financial Energy System

What is BICS?

BICS helps analyze personal spending not only by amount, but also by the reason behind the purchase.

The same purchase can belong to different categories depending on the reason.

For example, sneakers can be Basic, Signal, or Chaos depending on the reason for buying them.

BICS Categories

Basic

Essential basic expenses.

Example: apartment rent.

Invest

Development, education, health, useful tools, and assets.

Example: JavaScript course.

Chaos

Impulsive and unplanned spending.

Example: spontaneous food delivery.

Signal

Purchases for status, image, and self-presentation.

Example: expensive watch.

Transaction Examples

  • Apartment rent - essential expense - Basic
  • JavaScript course - investment in development - Invest
  • Spontaneous food delivery - impulsive decision - Chaos
  • Expensive watch - purchase for image - Signal
